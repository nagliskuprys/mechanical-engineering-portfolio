## Overview

The project included the design and calculation of a gearbox, chain drive, shafts, bearings, housing and supporting structure.

## Software

- SolidWorks
- Autodesk Inventor
- Microsoft Excel

## Project Scope

- Electric motor selection
- Gear transmission design
- Chain drive design
- Shaft design and calculations
- Bearing selection
- Key connection design
- Housing design
- Coupling selection
- Technical drawing preparation
- Tolerance and fit specification
